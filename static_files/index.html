Controls:

  • 'W A S D' to move
  • 'Left Click' to shoot
  • 'R' to reload
  • 'P' to pause
  • 'M' to display minimap
  • '1 2' to switch weapons

Hints:

  • When your gun is darkred, you have to reload.
  • When your gun is orange, you are reloading and cannot shoot.
  • Destroy boxes for health and ammo pickups.
  • Health and ammo pickups disappear when a new round starts. Be sure to pick them up before defeating the last enemy.
  • Enemies may sometimes get stuck behind objects. Use this time to heal your sheilds then hunt then down with the minimap!
  • The Enemies don't think highly of one another and can damage each other. Use this to your advantage!
